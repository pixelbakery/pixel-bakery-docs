# Rejection Email Templates

On this page you'll find some commonly used message templates we send to 'no-go' applicants. It's important to remember that these are only _templates_. Each applicant is special, so it's critical to remember to tailor each message specifically for that applicant.

## No Interview Applicant Rejection Email

> XXX, 
>
> Thank you for taking the time to apply at Pixel Bakery. Unfortunately, we have decided to proceed in a different direction for the position you applied for. 
>
> However, we will keep your application on file for consideration if there is a future opening that may be a fit for you. We do appreciate you taking the time to apply and encourage you to apply for other openings in the future. 
>
> Please let me know if you have any questions, and thank you again for your interest in Pixel Bakery. 
>
>Sincerely, 
>
>– Maddie

## Interview Applicant Rejection Email
> XXX,
>
>Thank you for taking the time to apply and interview with Pixel Bakery. Unfortunately, we have decided to proceed in a different direction for the position you applied for.
>
>Your interview was great and you're extremely qualified, but we determined that you're not quite the right fit at this moment. It is a decision we didn't make easily because you are really a strong candidate with a wonderful personality.
>
>However, we will keep your application on file for consideration if there is a future opening that may be a fit for you. We do appreciate you taking the time to interview with us and encourage you to apply for other openings in the future.
>
>Please let me know if you have any questions, and thank you again for your interest in Pixel Bakery.
>
>Sincerely,
>
>– Maddie
