# How To: Offboard an Employee from Calendly

On this page you'll learn how to offboard an employee from Calendly.

**GENERAL NOTE:** Leadership are the only employees who have access to Calendly. You'll need to be an 'Admin' to remove an employee from Calendly.

## Step 1. Login to Calendly using the credentials on 1Pass.

![Alt text](/img/Offboarding_Calendly_1.png 'title')

## Step 2. Under 'account' press 'admin managment.'

![Alt text](/img/Offboarding_Calendly_2.png 'title')

## Step 3. under 'all people' find the person you'd like to remove and press 'remove.'

![Alt text](/img/Offboarding_Calendly_3.png 'title')
