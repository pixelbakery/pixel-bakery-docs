# How To: Offboard an Employee from the Studio

There are 2 steps for offboarding an employee from the studio.

## Step 1. Key Fob.

Make sure the employee returns their key fob that grants them access to the building. Once returned, hand into Laura Orth, the Events Coordinator at Turbine Flats.

## Step 2. Parking Permit.

Make sure the employee returns their Turbine Flats Parking Permit they use to park in the parking lot. Once returned, hand into Larua Orth, the Events Coordinator at Turbine Flats.
