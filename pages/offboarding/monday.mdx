# How To: Offboard an Employee from Monday.com

On this page you'll learn how to offboard an employee from Monday.com.

**GENERAL NOTE:** You'll need to be an 'Admin' to deactivate an employee's account on Monday.com.

## Step 1. Login to Monday.com.

![Alt text](/img/Offboarding_Monday_1.png 'title')

## Step 2. Click your profile picture in the bottom left corner. Navigate to 'admin.' 

![Alt text](/img/Offboarding_Monday_2.png 'title')

## Step 3. Under admin click 'users.'

![Alt text](/img/Offboarding_Monday_3.png 'title')

## Step 4. Scroll to the employee you'd like to deactivate.

![Alt text](/img/Offboarding_Monday_4.png 'title')

## Step 5. Press the 3 dots to the right of the employee you'd like to deactivate.

![Alt text](/img/Offboarding_Monday_5.png 'title')

## Step 6. Press 'deactivate user.'

![Alt text](/img/Offboarding_Monday_6.png 'title')
