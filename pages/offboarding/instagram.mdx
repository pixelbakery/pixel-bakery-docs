# How To: Offboard an Employee from Instagram

To offboard an employee from Instagram you need to change the password to the Pixel Bakery Instagram Account. 

## Step 1. Login to the Pixel Bakery Account and head to Pixel Bakery's profile.

![Alt text](/img/Offboarding_Instagram_1.png 'title')

## Step 2. At the top of the page click the icon to the right of 'add tools'

![Alt text](/img/Offboarding_Instagram_2.png 'title')

## Step 3. Click 'change password.'

![Alt text](/img/Offboarding_Instagram_3.png 'title')

## Step 4. Enter the old password and add the new password.

**GENERAL NOTE:** Make sure to add the new password in 1Password.

![Alt text](/img/Offboarding_Instagram_4.png 'title')
